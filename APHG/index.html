Giving Sustainablity to Developing Countries

Mission: Giving clean energy to those who need it

Home

Many countries are currently developing. An important part of this demographic change is energy in the form of electricity. But, the cheapeast and most used way to gain this electricity is through the burning of fossil fuels. This releases green house gasses, causes global warming changes and hurts our wonderful and natural environment. Many developing countries are located in Africa. This continent is centered around the equator and thefore receives a lot of harvestable energy from the sun each year. Our mission is to take advantage of this to give clean harm-free electricity to serve these countries in their developing stages. According to the Worldbank.org electricty is very vital to economic development in countries.


Why Donate?

One of the goals made at the beginning of this generation was to reach a sustainable environment. Giving these developing countries a head start with renewable and sustainable resources will help these countries reach this goal. Donating to this cause will not only:

  1. Give you nice tax reductions
  2. Put unused money to a great cause
  3. Save the environment
  4. Help the world

but it will also help other countries such as the United States of America, and other developed countries reach this green energy point as well. Your donations will be used with everyone in mind, and they can come back to help your country as well.

Funding Plan

We use donations to buy these first solar panels. We install them on solar farms and sell the energy to African Power Suppliers. The money we recieve from that is only used to buy even more solar panels. We have started small, but we will get bigger. To start, we need about 10,000 dollars, we need it for transport and the cost of solar panels. At first, we will not install them on farms. After out NGO has grown a bit more, we will be able to purchase african land. But, for now, we will give them to houseolds and farms, not owned by us.
